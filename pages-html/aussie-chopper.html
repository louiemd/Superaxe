COMMON FEATURES

The Aussie Chopper wood splitter is available in 5 different models – the WS75, WS100, WS150, WS3150 AND WS4150 T-Blade. All models feature the following:
  • Designed and manufactured in Australia. Whitlands Engineering only uses quality components sourced through reputable local distributors and manufactures. All machines are built from scratch in our workshop in north east Victoria.
  • Patented blade design for superior splitting performance: Vertical sliding action easily accommodates different lengths of wood up to 450mm
  • Rugged heavy-duty construction – minimal maintenance required
  • Back saving log lifter and ideal bench height for advanced ergonomic comfort (except on WS75)
  • Easy and safe to use – innovative single or double handed control system that enhances safety by keeping hands away from moving blade. This system is completely Workcover compliant.
  • Two-year warranty on all parts and construction
  • Reliable and fuel efficient Subaru Robin or Honda engines all backed by 3 years manufactures warranty
  • Remote powered or 3pl permanent base units available

  The second smallest Aussie Chopper in the range, the WS100 is the ideal solution for processing domestic volumes of firewood. Fitted with a 6hp engine, and a 100kg capacity cable operated log lifter, the Aussie Chopper WS100 is surprisingly powerful. The WS100 features an innovative single-handed ‘on command’ control system that is compliant to Workcover guidelines. The 6hp engine delivers a whopping 19 tons of splitting force and 14-second cycle time

Featuring the innovative and efficient pivoting blade the Superaxe is the ideal solution for processing larger volumes of firewood for medium-large scale commercial, farm and agro forestry operations. The Superaxe is available in 3 different models – the WS300, WS400 and WS450.
